Our Dental Services

We offer a comprehensive range of dental services to meet the needs of patients of all ages. Our goal is to help you achieve and maintain optimal oral health through preventive, restorative, and cosmetic treatments.

Preventive Care

Regular check-ups, professional cleanings, and fluoride treatments to maintain optimal oral health and prevent dental problems before they start.

Includes: Routine exams, professional cleanings, fluoride treatments, sealants, oral cancer screenings, and personalized home care recommendations.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Transform your smile with teeth whitening, veneers, bonding, and other cosmetic procedures designed to enhance your natural beauty.

Includes: Professional teeth whitening, porcelain veneers, composite bonding, gum contouring, and smile makeovers.

Restorative Care

Comprehensive solutions including fillings, crowns, bridges, and dentures to restore function and appearance to damaged or missing teeth.

Includes: Tooth-colored fillings, ceramic crowns, dental bridges, partial and full dentures, and same-day restorations.

Orthodontics

Modern orthodontic solutions to straighten teeth and correct bite issues for patients of all ages.

Includes: Traditional braces, clear aligners (Invisalign), retainers, and early intervention for children.

Pediatric Dentistry

Specialized care for children, creating positive dental experiences from an early age.

Includes: Child-friendly exams, preventive treatments, behavior management, and education on proper oral hygiene habits.

Oral Surgery

Expert surgical procedures including extractions, implant placement, and other oral surgical treatments.

Includes: Tooth extractions, wisdom teeth removal, dental implants, bone grafting, and soft tissue procedures.

Insurance & Payment Information

Accepted Insurance Plans

We accept most major dental insurance plans, including:

  • Aetna Dental
  • Cigna Dental
  • Dental Blue
  • Delta Dental
  • MetLife Dental
  • UnitedHealthcare Dental

Our team will verify your coverage and explain your benefits before any treatment begins.

Payment Options

We offer flexible payment options to make dental care accessible to everyone:

  • Major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover)
  • Debit cards
  • Flexible Spending Accounts (FSA) and Health Savings Accounts (HSA)
  • Financing options through CareCredit®
  • Discount plan for uninsured patients

We also offer payment plans for larger treatments to help you manage costs.
