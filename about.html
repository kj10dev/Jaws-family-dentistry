About Our Team

Meet Our Dedicated Dental Professionals

At JAWS Family Dentistry, we're proud to have a team of highly skilled, compassionate professionals who are committed to providing exceptional dental care to our patients.

Our doctors have years of experience and are dedicated to staying current with the latest advancements in dentistry through ongoing education and training. They approach each patient with empathy, patience, and a genuine desire to help them achieve optimal oral health.

Our support staff is equally committed to your comfort and satisfaction. From the moment you walk through our doors, you'll be greeted with warmth and professionalism by our friendly receptionists, hygienists, and assistants.

Our Mission & Values

Mission: To provide comprehensive, compassionate dental care that enhances the health, confidence, and well-being of our patients and community.

Values:

  • Compassion: We treat every patient with kindness, respect, and understanding.
  • Excellence: We strive for the highest standards in clinical care and patient service.
  • Integrity: We are honest, ethical, and transparent in all our interactions.
  • Innovation: We embrace new technologies and techniques to improve patient outcomes.
  • Community: We are committed to giving back and improving the health of our local community.

Our Dental Team

Dr. Sarah Johnson

General Dentist & Practice Owner

Dr. Johnson has over 15 years of experience in family dentistry. She graduated from the University of California, San Francisco School of Dentistry and is passionate about preventive care and patient education.

Dr. Michael Chen

Orthodontist & Cosmetic Specialist

Dr. Chen specializes in orthodontics and cosmetic dentistry. He completed his residency at New York University College of Dentistry and is known for his gentle approach and artistic eye for smile design.

Dr. Emily Rodriguez

Pediatric Dentist

Dr. Rodriguez specializes in treating children and adolescents. She creates a fun, welcoming environment that helps kids feel comfortable and excited about dental visits.

Dr. David Wilson

Oral Surgeon & Implant Specialist

Dr. Wilson has extensive experience in oral surgery and dental implants. He's known for his precision, calm demeanor, and ability to make complex procedures seem simple.
